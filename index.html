Who are we?

Anything but a team of grade 7s

Hi, we are the Silent Symphonies. A team of 10 grade 7s. Trying everyday to break the barrier breaking us, and auditorily impaired people apart from what we use daily known as music. With approximately 1.5 billion people worldwide facing auditory impairment, the inability to hear sounds can make the enjoyment of music becomes a significant challenge. This exclusion often leaves individuals with a sense of being left out, devoid of a solution. At the core of our belief is the conviction that everyone, irrespective of their auditory abilities, should have the opportunity not only to appreciate but also to actively participate in the creation of music. Our mission is to break down barriers and provide inclusive avenues for the joy and expression that music brings, ensuring that nobody is left behind in the transformative power of sound. And the problem with the old bone conduction is that it can drown out your audio, it is very inconsistent. And most importantly, extremely uncomfortable. Especially with hefty price of hearing aids(8,000$). We believe that our project will change that. We wanted to make a headphone that would help deaf people enjoy music for 80 times less the price using other senses. Which later led to the creation of Vibrationz and AuraX. We believe that our innovative solution will allow deaf people to enjoy music.
